Every Saturday City Tours

Explore the hidden gems and iconic landmarks of your city every Saturday with Kay Hikers Club's guided tours. Open to all fitness levels, these tours offer a fantastic way to discover your city on foot, learn fascinating facts, and connect with fellow adventurers. Don't worry about transportation! Buses are stationed at various muster points (Gwarimpa Axis: Galadima gate, Lugbe Axis: Lugbe Car Wash) to ensure everyone can participate. After the tour, enjoy a delightful post-hike meal of delicious fish and drinks, fostering camaraderie and shared experiences. Buses will again be available at the sub-muster points for those who need transportation back.

Wednesday Coffee Hangouts

Engage in insightful conversations with fellow members, guest speakers, and local experts on various topics related to travel, exploration, culture, and personal growth. These interactive sessions offer a unique opportunity to learn, share experiences, and expand your horizons. Grab your favorite coffee, come prepared to ask questions, and be ready to embark on a journey of intellectual discovery.

Themed Parties for Celebrations

Dive into the festive spirit with Kay Hikers Club's vibrant themed parties celebrating major holidays, cultural events, or even seasonal occasions. These lively gatherings offer a platform to connect with fellow members, indulge in delicious food and drinks, and create lasting memories. Get ready to experience cultural performances, themed games, and exciting activities that capture the essence of each celebration
